# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 065
**Title:** The Same Night
**Slug:** time-loop
**Category:** Supernatural/Bizarre

## The Death (One Sentence)
An obsessive planner trapped in a time loop keeps dying at the same Prague hostel night after night, each attempt to escape the loop ending in a new and creative death until she finally breaks - and discovers that dying was always the only way out.

## Tagline
"Same night. Different death. Every time."

## Themes
1. The illusion of control - satirizes people who believe they can plan their way out of anything
2. The horror of repetition - what happens when you're trapped in your own worst day
3. The paradox of choice - more options don't mean better outcomes

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 45% (1-100)
- Horror: 35%
- Satire: 20%

## Target Reader
Anyone who's ever been a compulsive planner, people who've felt trapped in repetitive patterns, Groundhog Day fans, those who've wondered what they'd do with infinite chances.

## Comparison Titles
- "Happy Death Day" meets "Russian Doll" with backpacker culture

## Content Warnings
- Repeated death scenes (various methods)
- Psychological horror
- Suicide ideation (as escape attempts)
- Mental deterioration
- Existential crisis
